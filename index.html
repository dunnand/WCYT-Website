About
Where music is the point

The Point 91 FM is your independent music source and alternative to commercial radio, broadcasting from Homestead High School in Fort Wayne, Indiana. Where music is the point. Everything you hear is made by students.

We broadcast on 91.1 FM and stream online 24/7. Our students handle everything: on-air talent, production, sports broadcasting, and content creation.
