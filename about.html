Who We Are

Fort Wayne's student-run radio station

The Point 91 FM is based in Fort Wayne, Indiana, dedicated to serving the community with fresh tracks and a genuine alternative to commercial radio. Founded in 1994 and located inside Homestead High School, we are entirely student run and operated.

We cover all things radio and TV related at Homestead, from live sports broadcasts to interviews with local artists. Everything you hear is made by students.

Our Mission

Music is the point

We exist to give students a real platform to develop skills in broadcasting, production, and on-air performance while delivering something genuinely different to listeners in Fort Wayne.

No playlists curated by an algorithm. No corporate formatting. Just students who care about music, bringing you something worth listening to.
